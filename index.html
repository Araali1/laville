Mwiru Holdings SMC - Ltd is committed to ensuring that all of the processes followed are designed to deliver a high quality outcome for the clients and the industry in general. Our processes are founded on the framework of Integrated Management Systems (IMS) – (QMS & EMS) with a focus on excellence as the heart of it.

We understand that our customers are judged by the highest standards of quality and service. The facilities they care for are expected to be hygienic and environmentally sound an ever-changing, never-ending challenge. We, at Mwiru Holdings - SMC Ltd promise to be a full partner in helping our customers meet and exceed these expectations.

As major supporters of the cleaning and property services sector and are extremely interested in the promotion of good environmental choices by our customers.
